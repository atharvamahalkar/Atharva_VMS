DOCUMENT_TYPE: policy
VENDOR_NAME: Internal Procurement Policy

SECTION: Vendor Selection
Vendors must be evaluated based on quality, cost, and reliability.
A minimum score of 75 percent is required.

SECTION: Payment Policy
Payments should follow agreed contract terms.
Advance payment should not exceed 20 percent.

SECTION: Compliance Rules
All vendors must comply with ISO standards.
Non-compliance may result in disqualification.

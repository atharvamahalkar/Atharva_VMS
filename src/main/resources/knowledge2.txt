 2. Food Services  
Vendor may sell the following food and/or beverages and/or provide the following food services ("Services") for District events on those dates, times, sites, and locations specified in a separate Vendor Services Order Form, each of which shall be attached to this Agreement:  
- Grilled chicken and beef BBQ platters with sides (potato salad, coleslaw, corn on the cob)  
- Fresh vegetable skewers and salads  
- Non-alcoholic beverages (soda, water, iced tea, lemonade)  
- Dessert items (cookies, brownies)  
All items prepared fresh on-site using District-approved facilities. Vendor shall not sell alcoholic beverages. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/7993660/fd75c2a9-4c5e-483e-a058-2baac35d4304/knowledge2.txt)

 3. Compliance with Laws and Regulations  
Vendor will comply with all applicable laws and regulations governing the sale of food and/or beverages, including the Health and Safety Code, the Education Code, City and County ordinances, and District policies and regulations. Food shall be prepared at a facility that meets or exceeds all state and local health standards (San Francisco County Health Permit SFHC-2026-04567), and shall be maintained at a safe and appropriate temperature at all times.  5. Facilities  
The District shall designate the specific location and maximum size for Vendor's booth, tent, cart, or other facility to be utilized by Vendor during each District event (maximum 20x20 ft space). District will designate acceptable use of District electricity (standard 20-amp outlet). Vendor shall be responsible for setting up and taking down such facilities and for clean-up of the area during and following each event. Vendor may begin setting up 2 hours before the scheduled event and must be completely set up before the start of the event. Vendor must disassemble its facility and complete clean-up and vacate the facility 1 hour after the conclusion of the event.
